Group to work, don't work to group

Groupease presents all the knowledge workers need to form compatible and productive groups in a simple interface. Don't waste time. Form the best groups quickly through informed decisions based on a member's skills, availability, and goals.

group_work

Channel your organized self

Groupease is organized into channels. Anyone can create a channel at any time for any purpose, so there is a private space for an organization, department, or classroom to meet.

search

Peer at your peers

Members complete brief per-channel profiles about their skills, interests, and goals. Groupease channels make it easy to see all their members, so potential teammates can learn about each other prior to forming groups.
